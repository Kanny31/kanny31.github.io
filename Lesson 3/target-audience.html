Target Audience

The targeted audience for this website is a very wide audience ranging from young adults to elderly. Specifically to the local areas in which the weather is being forcasted. Incomes include all poverty level up to upper-class citizens. The audience includes all categories of marital status, education level, etc. All demographics are targeted.

Persona 1

  • Age: 31
  • Income: $25,000 yearly
  • Education: Masters Degree
  • Experience: Checking the weather on their work computer. Wants to share pictures of the weather as his side hobby.
  • Marital Status: Engaged
  • How, When, and Where accessing site: This person checks the weather as soon as they get to work to see how their commute home will be. They tend to rely on the TV for the morning weather. Has a hobby of taking pictures during any kind of weather and sharing with everyone.

Persona 2

  • Age: 53
  • Income: $40,000 yearly
  • Education: Associates Degree
  • Experience: Persona 2 checks the weather on her iPad that her children gave her. She checks it periodically throughout the day while she is doing tasks and running errands.
  • Marital Status: Married
  • How, When, and Where accessing site: Persona 2 checks the weather in the morning before she goes to babysit her grandchilren. Then she checks it again before she leaves to go home and do her work-from-home job. She checks it as night before her nightly walk with her dogs.
