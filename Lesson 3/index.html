How's the Weather?

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide the forcast for the week in many different towns. Many people will be able to access it and see the weather in their area. They will also be able to report weather related stories from their area. Anyone who wishes to share photographs that they captured of the weather will also be upload those. People will also be able to contact All Things Weather main office to file a complaint or problem with the website. The content will be user friendly and accessible by all.
