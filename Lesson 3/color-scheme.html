Color Scheme

The following are the color pallete I choose for my All Things Weather website.

I choose these colors becasue they are calming and reminded me of the sky. They also seemed inviting and what you would see on a weather website. #95a3b3 will be my background color. #4b4e6d and #ffffff will be used for font colors. #84cc6 will be used for the home bar and #6b7baa will be used as an accent color.
