Welcome to our Humble Inn

Here at Temple Inn and Suites we want everyone to feel welcome and at home. We strive to provide the best experience around while you are serving in the house of The Lord. Our specialized hotel accommodations are near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. Our owner-operated Inn caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. Our hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company. We also offer services for wedding receptions and provide catering and rooms for the happy couple.
