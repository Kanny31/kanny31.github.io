Design Principles

White Space and Clean Design

Skooza used White Space and Clean Design principle well on their site. The website is clean and precise and it isn't over crowded. It's background is black but there is a lot of space surrounding the image. Once you open the webpage you know exactly what is for sale.

Skooza

Rule of Third

Facebook did a good job at exemplifying the rule of third design principle. Once you get to the login page your eye is drawn to the sign in. If you were to put a grid over it, the page would still have a nice layout. The sign in page isn't cluttered and it is easy to navigate and find what you are looking for.

Facebook

Hick's Law

Simths uses the Hick's Law design principle because their layout is simple. From the very start you know which website you are on. You can search for the items you want to buy, and it shows you what store you would be purcahsing from. There isn't a lot to read and you can find the store hours and the phone number quickly if you needed to.
